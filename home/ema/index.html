Emergency Services Portal

If you are facing a medical emergency, follow the guidelines below or contact us immediately.

Emergency Guidelines

  • Assess the Situation: If someone is in danger or experiencing a medical emergency, act quickly.
  • Call for Help: Use the contact numbers provided to get help immediately.
  • Provide First Aid: Administer first aid if you are trained, or follow instructions over the phone.
  • Stay Calm: Keep the patient comfortable and wait for emergency services to arrive.
